Delete Your MediRemind Account

Last updated: 18 May 2026

This page explains how to permanently delete your MediRemind account and all associated personal data, and what is removed versus retained. MediRemind is operated by Ovara Tech, Australia.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (recommended, instant)

  1. Open MediRemind on your phone.
  2. Tap Profile (bottom tab).
  3. Tap Settings.
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm by entering the one-time passcode sent to your email or phone.
  6. Your account is immediately deactivated and queued for permanent deletion.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer access the app (for example, you uninstalled it), send an email from the address associated with your MediRemind account:

Email info@mediremind.com.au

Subject line: "Delete my MediRemind account". We will verify your identity, action the request, and confirm by reply within 7 business days (maximum 30 days as required by the Australian Privacy Act).

What gets deleted

  • Your name, email address, phone number, date of birth, profile photo
  • All medication records, dosages, schedules, supply counts, dose history, notes
  • Scanned eScript / prescription images you saved
  • Reminder settings and push notification tokens
  • Family-share links and tokens you created (revoked immediately)
  • Authentication credentials (password hash, OAuth links)
  • Account profile and any additional profiles you created

What is retained, and for how long

DataRetentionReason
Anonymised crash logs & diagnosticsUp to 90 days, then deletedOperational reliability; no longer identifies you
Encrypted backupsUp to 30 days from deletionDisaster recovery; purged on the rolling cycle
Legal & financial records (if any)As required by Australian lawTax / audit obligations — does not include health data
This action is permanent. Once deleted, your medication history, dose logs and reminders cannot be recovered. If you only want to take a break, you can simply sign out instead.
