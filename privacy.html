MediRemind Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 May 2026 · Effective: 18 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MediRemind ("MediRemind", "we", "us") collects, uses, stores and discloses your personal information when you use the MediRemind mobile application and related services (the "Service"). MediRemind is operated by Ovara Tech, Australia. Contact: info@mediremind.com.au.

We comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). By using MediRemind you agree to the practices described below.

1. What MediRemind does

MediRemind is a personal medication reminder app. It helps you record medications that have been prescribed to you by your healthcare provider, schedule reminders to take them, track supply, log doses, scan Australian eScript QR codes for data entry, and optionally share a read-only view with family carers. MediRemind does not provide medical advice, diagnose conditions, recommend medications, dispense prescriptions, or function as a registered medical device.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and push tokens — a unique device identifier and push notification token so we can deliver reminders.
  • App interactions — taps, reminder responses, dose-logged events, used to make the app work and improve reliability.
  • Crash logs & diagnostics — anonymised error reports, performance metrics, server logs used to keep the service running.

2.3 Information we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your precise location.
  • We do not collect contacts, calendar, browsing history, SMS, or files outside the app.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers and we do not show ads.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide the core reminder, tracking, scanning and family-share features.
  • To authenticate you (email OTP via Twilio, email delivery via Resend, optional Google OAuth sign-in).
  • To send you reminder notifications you have scheduled.
  • To diagnose crashes and operational issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

5. Data security

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS / TLS.
  • Account passwords are hashed with industry-standard algorithms; we never store plain-text passwords.
  • OTP codes are short-lived and single-use.
  • Backend databases sit behind authenticated APIs with audit logging.

6. Data retention

  • Account & health data: retained while your account is active.
  • On account deletion: permanently removed within 30 days.
  • Crash logs & anonymised diagnostics: retained up to 90 days.
  • Backups: rolling encrypted backups purged within 30 days of account deletion.

7. Your rights & how to delete your account

Under the Australian Privacy Principles you have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information.

  • Delete your account in-app: Open MediRemind → Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Confirm with OTP.
  • Request deletion by email: info@mediremind.com.au from the email on your account.
  • Request a copy of your data: email the same address; we'll respond within 30 days.
  • See also our Account Deletion page.

8. Children

MediRemind is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, contact info@mediremind.com.au and we will delete it.

9. International transfers

Some service providers (above) are based in the United States or other countries. By using MediRemind you consent to your information being transferred to and processed in those jurisdictions. We require all sub-processors to apply security and confidentiality standards comparable to Australian law.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified in-app or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the current version.
